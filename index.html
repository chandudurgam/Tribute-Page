Dr. B. R. Ambedkar

The Man who tried to turn the Wheel of the Law toward social justice for all

Here's Is the Timeline of The Dr. B. R. Ambedkar's Life:

  • 1891 - On 14 April Born At Mhow(Madhyapradesh, India).
  • 1913 - B.A in economics and political science from University of Bombay.
  • 1915 - M.A. in Economics from Columbia University in New York City.
  • 1921 - M.Sc. in Economics from London School of Economics.
  • 1922 - Barrister-at-law from Gray's Inn, London UK
  • 1952 - LL.D.(Honoris Causa) Columbia University, New York
  • 1953 - D.Litt.(Honoris Causa) Osmania University, India
  • 1956 - Passed Away(aged 65) at Delhi, India On 6 Dec
  • 1990 - Dr. B. R. Ambedkar is posthumously awarded India’s highest civilian award, the Bharat Ratna
